# Challenge 1: Clinical Priority System

## Business Problem
A healthcare provider processes thousands of diagnostic reports daily. Some reports contain urgent findings that require immediate attention, but currently all reports are reviewed manually.

The organization wants to identify high-priority cases faster by analyzing patient and clinical data.

---

## Data
Here is an example of given clinical data, including:

- `age`: Patient age
- `sex`: Patient gender
- `chest_pain_type`: Type of chest pain
- `resting_blood_pressure`: Blood pressure level
- `cholesterol`: Cholesterol level
- `max_heart_rate`: Maximum heart rate achieved
- `target`: Indicates presence of heart disease

Use this as a frame of reference when looking through the kaggle dataset.

Name of Dataset: Heart Disease Dataset

## Tasks
- Analyze patterns that indicate high-risk patients
- Engineer features that may improve prediction performance
- Build a model to classify high-risk vs low-risk cases
- Evaluate model performance
- Identify the most important factors contributing to risk
