# Challenge 3: Product Recommendation Engine

## Business Problem
An online retail company wants to increase engagement and sales by recommending relevant products to users.

Currently, recommendations are not personalized.

---

## Data
Here is an example of given user interaction data, including:

- `user_id`: Unique user identifier
- `product_id`: Unique product identifier
- `category`: Product category
- `event_type`: Interaction type (view, add-to-cart, purchase)
- `timestamp`: Time of interaction

Use this as a frame of reference when looking through the kaggle dataset.

Name of Dataset: RetailRocket E-Commerce Dataset  
KAGGLE CLI DOWNLOAD: kaggle datasets download -d retailrocket/ecommerce-dataset  
Kaggle Link: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/retailrocket/ecommerce-dataset

---

## Tasks
- Analyze user behavior and product interaction patterns
- Identify relationships between users and products
- Develop a method to recommend products
- Evaluate recommendation quality
- Identify factors that influence user engagement
