# Challenge 6: Student Retention Prediction

## Business Problem
An online learning platform has observed that many students drop out before completing their courses.

The company wants to identify at-risk students early to improve retention.

---

## Data
Here is an example of given student activity data, including:

- `time_spent_per_week`: Time spent on platform
- `assignments_completed`: Number of completed assignments
- `quiz_scores`: Performance on quizzes
- `last_login_days_ago`: Days since last activity
- `forum_activity`: Participation in discussions
- `dropped_out`: Whether the student dropped out

Use this as a frame of reference when looking through the kaggle dataset.

Name of Dataset: xAPI-Edu-Data  
KAGGLE CLI DOWNLOAD: kaggle datasets download -d aljarah/xapi-edu-data  
Kaggle Link: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/aljarah/xapi-edu-data

---

## Tasks
- Analyze behavioral differences between retained and dropped students
- Identify key indicators of disengagement
- Build a model to predict dropout risk
- Evaluate model performance
- Identify the most important factors influencing retention
